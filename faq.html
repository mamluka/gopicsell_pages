What is picSell?

picSell helps you sell stuff you no longer need in under a minute.

What are your fees?

10% per item (we deduct all fees before quoting you a price range; what you see is what you get).

How do I sell my stuff?

It's simple:

  1. Take 3 photos of the product you want to sell
  2. Email the pics to sales@gopicsell.com along with the brand and model (e.g., Apple magic mouse) and add a short description if you like
  3. We’ll send you a price range. Once you approve, we’ll list your product in a few marketplaces (e.g., Amazon, eBay, Etsy)
  4. Once your product sells, we’ll email you a pre-paid shipping label
  5. We schedule a pick up or you can leave the package at a drop off location
  6. We pay you!
What insurance/guarantees do I have once my product is shipped?

First, we will only send you a shipping label once we have received payment from the buyer. We will then email you a prepaid, trackable and insured shipping label. Once your package is scanned by the carrier, it is insured for loss and damage.

How long does it take to get paid?

As soon as you ship your item, we will credit your picSell account with the agreed amount. In about 14 days, you will receive payment through PayPal, direct bank deposit or check (whichever you prefer).

How long do I have to ship my item?

This is an important one. You have 2 business days to have your package picked up or to drop it off at a drop-off location. If your product is not shipped after two business days your order is subject to cancellation and your picSell account may be terminated. In order to sell your items more quickly, we promise buyers their order will be shipped within 2 business days.

What happens once my product is sold?

Once we receive payment for your product, we’ll send you a pre-paid, trackable, insured shipping label. You just need to package your item in accordance with the packaging guidelines and we can either schedule a carrier pick up (for a fee) or you can drop it off at a convenient drop-off location.

What are the guidelines for taking photos of my item?
  • Plain, uncluttered backdrop
  • Show close-up details
  • Diffused lighting shows the details
  • Camera flash is off
  • Tripod for a sharper focus
  • Item sized to fill the frame
  • Accurate colors
  • It's size is shown
  • Large image size supports zoom and enlarge functions
  • Multiple angles and close-ups show important details

    • You can also see the eBay photo guidelines.

What happens if my package gets lost in the mail?

All packages are insured and trackable. We will work directly with the mail carrier if your package is lost.

How do I delete data off my phone or computer?

You can either do an Internet search on how to wipe your device or we have a service where picSell can erase your data for a fee. If you’re interested in this services, please email us at 'sales@gopicsell.com'.

What shipping carrier do you use?

In order to sell your item as fast as possible, we try and keep shipping costs to a minimum. Therefore, we use several different carriers depending on the circumstances. Your pre-paid shipping label will indicate the carrier name.

What are the fees?

The price we quote you is the price you will receive when your product is sold. We deduct our fee (which is 10% of the transaction value) before we send you the quote.

Who pays for shipping?

The buyer pays shipping unless the product is defective or is not as described in the photos or listing.

What if my product is returned?

The buyer pays return shipping fees unless the item is defective or not as described in the photos or listing. If the item is damaged during shipment, we will work the carrier to resolve. If you would like, picSell will realist returned items that are in good condition.

How do I get paid?

It’s simple. We pay you via PayPal, direct bank deposit or a check, your choice. In order to pay you via PayPal or bank deposit, we will need a few additional account details.

Can I sell everything on picSell?

Almost everything with a value of at least $50. For example, phones, tablets, computers, espresso machines, home improvement items (kitchen range hoods, faucets, etc.), brand name accessories (sunglasses, bags, shoes, watches), cameras, cars, A/Cs, etc. Obviously we don’t sell any illegal items and we reserve the right to not sell an item for any reason. Keep in mind that not all items make economic sense to sell - for example an item that has a value of $100 but costs $75 to ship.

What are the packaging guidelines?
  • Carefully wrap the package and attached the pre-paid label
  • Prepare a professional, clean package. Use new shipping/packing materials intended for packing items like yours. Avoid the use of drying materials or similar substances. Seal all shipments tightly and be sure to wipe away any dust or debris that may have collected on the item before packing for delivery.
  • Include the packing slip emailed to you from picSell with your item.
What if I want someone else to package my item?

No problem. We understand. For a fee, we can arrange for your items to be picked up, packaged and mailed.

I have a question that isn't listed here.
