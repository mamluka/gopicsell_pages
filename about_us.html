What are we all about?

We simplify your life just a little bit. picSell helps you sell stuff you no longer need in under a minute. Through our unique pricing engine, simplified process and seamless user experience, selling your stuff is easy, fast and profitable! We know everyone can (1) take a picture and (2) email it to us. That’s all there is to it. We do the rest and you get paid.

Our Story

It all started with coffee. A good friend upgraded his espresso machine. I had just sold my old iPhone and suggested he sell his used espresso machine. He said he couldn’t be bothered so I sold it for him. $1000 richer, he gave me his old iPhone, camera and watch to sell. All sold within 3 days. A new business was born.

Why use picSell?

Studies show that most people have between $1,000-$2,000 worth of items they no longer need lying around gathering dust. But who wants to bother with selling these items online? There’s the effort of listing, answering emails, dealing with seller ratings, payment issues and the big S (shipping!) – it’s too time consuming and frustrating. Now, imagine you don’t need to do any of these things – picSell can do it all for you. The result is no frustration, no time investment, reduced clutter and extra cash to spend! It’s easy, fast and profitable. Give us a try – we guarantee that once you sell 1 item through us, you’ll be back to sell more!
